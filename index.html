Sample Work: Trendcompass

In the following you can have a first look at my own creation of the Trendcompass, which I have first seen at the website of HSBC With this Trendcompass you have the ability to analyze financial stock data from the past and thus discover possible price trends in the stock market. The interactive Chart clearly shows the relative strength according to Levy and the momentum of S&P 500 shares. The Interactivtiy of the Chart, thus zooming in and out, as well as draging the chart to different positions is possible due to the Usage of the Vega-Altair Module in Python. Please take into strong account that the past performance of an underlying base value is not a reliable indicator of future performance. The Purpose of this Implementation is mainly to showcase my expertise in working with Data and my Interest in the Financial Industry.
